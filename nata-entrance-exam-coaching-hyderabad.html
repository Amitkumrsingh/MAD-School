NATA ENTRANCE EXAM

Understanding the ins and outs…

NATA stands for National Aptitude Test in Architecture. It is a competitive exam conducted all across India to get admission to undergraduate architecture programs. This national-level exam is conducted by the Council of Architecture (COA).

NATA exam is conducted to evaluate the candidate's:


Architectural aptitude


Drawing


Observation skills


Critical thinking


Aesthetic sensitivity


NATA scores are accepted by various architecture colleges across India as a fundamental criterion for admission to their B.Arch. programs.

There are other exams such as JEE2, CEPT, and many more that are similar to NATA that are accepted by particular colleges in India.


NATA Exam Eligibility 2024:


There is no age limit when it comes to attempting the NATA entrance exam.


NATA Exam Pattern 2024:


Mode of test for NATA exam 2024 is online (Computer based test).

Duration of NATA exam 2024 is 3 hours.

NATA exam has one section that tests general aptitude.

Total mark for which the NATA exam is conducted is 200.

Total number of questions asked in NATA exam is 125.

There are no negative markings in NATA exam 2024.

The National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA) exam pattern was changed in 2020. Even though the COA (Council of Architecture) changes the exam pattern periodically, for the 2020 batch there was a major change in the NATA exam pattern.

The new NATA exam pattern focuses more on the aptitude section and the drawing section was removed from the exam. This change was made to make the exam more aligned with the present curriculum and learning outcomes of B.Arch programs. And, one more reason was to make the exam more accessible to students from all backgrounds, science or commerce.

What was the previous NATA exam pattern? Is the old pattern coming back?


NATA 2024 Attempts:


According to the COA, students are allowed to appear for up to 3 NATA tests.

Case 1 - If the candidate appears for 1 test, that particular score will be taken into consideration.

Case 2 - If the candidate appears for 2 tests, the best of 2 scores will be taken into consideration.

Case 3 - If the candidate appears for all 3 tests, the average of the 2 best scores will be taken into consideration.

FAQs

Find your answers…

1. What is NATA exam?

The National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA) is a national-level entrance exam conducted as criteria to get admission to undergraduate courses in architecture. The Council of Architecture (CoA) conducts the exam and the score is accepted by most of the top architecture colleges in India.

2. Is coaching necessary for Nata examination?

Coaching for the NATA exam is not necessary if you have a strong foundation in architectural concepts and if you are good with PCM. However, coaching will provide you guidance and practice depending on the latest exam trends. Coaching will be beneficial for students with time constraints. Coaching will help you improve in the areas you are weak and work on them.

3. When will the NATA 2024 dates be announced?

The NATA 2024 dates have not yet been announced. However, the application process usually opens in September/October of the previous year, and the exam dates are announced in December/January of the same year. The dates can be expected to be announced sometime in December 2023 or January 2024.

To be updated about the dates and latest notifications, you can visit the “All you need to know about NATA” page or the official NATA website https://nata.in/

4. Can I appear for JEE2 and CEPT if I am attempting NATA?

Yes, you can attempt for JEE2 and CEPT while appearing for NATA. You just need to make sure you are prepared enough to take all the tests.

5. Does NATA have negative marking?

No, NATA exam 2024 does not have negative marking.

6. Is NATA compulsory for architecture?

Yes, NATA is compulsory for architecture. Your NATA score is the basic criteria for selection in architecture colleges. Your NATA score and your higher secondary examination marks are added up and taken into consideration for the admission process.

7. Is NATA exam tough?

NATA exam is considered to be moderately difficult. The difficulty level of the NATA exam depends on your strengths and weaknesses.
