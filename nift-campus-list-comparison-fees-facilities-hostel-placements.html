NIFT (National Institute of Fashion Technology) specializes in offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs focused on fashion and design education. There are NIFT campuses across multiple cities in India and is known for its curriculum and industry-oriented approach.

The first NIFT campus was inaugurated in Delhi in 1985 and it was declared a statutory institute in 2006. Students have to take up the NIFT entrance exam to get admission into one of the campuses and the seats are allotted based on their performance in the exam.

NIFT Courses

NIFT offers 5 programs, of which 2 are undergraduate programs and 3 are master programs.

Under Graduate Programs

  • Bachelor of Design (B.Des), 4 years
  • Bachelor of Fashion Technology (B. Ftech), 4 years

Master Programs

  • Master of Design (M.Des), 2 years
  • Master of Fashion Technology (M. Ftech), 2 years
  • Master of Fashion Management (MFM), 2 years

Apart from this there are Ph.D. (Part time and Full time) and Diploma programmes as well that are offered in some NIFT campuses.

NIFT Scholarships

There are two scholarships offered in NIFT:

- “SARTHAK” NIFT FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE SCHEME - This scholarship is offered for students who are from low-income backgrounds but have an exceptional academic record. Students can get up to 100% waiver in tuition fees based on the income of the family. You can go through the brochure available on the NIFT website to get more details on the eligibility and process involved in the scholarship.

- “UDAAN”- NIFT MEANS – CUM - MERIT SCHEME FOR FOREIGN STUDIES - The purpose of this scholarship is to provide financial assistance to students who have a low-income background and want to pursue their design studies abroad. From tuition fees to travel tickets to abroad, students can get 100% fee waiver on those. Refer to the brochure available on the official NIFT website to check on the eligibility and the process involved.
