Yes, Mad School provides guidance in creating fashion portfolios for NIFT, NID, and other design colleges. Our faculties help in creating a strong and impressive portfolio with which you can apply to national and international colleges.

There are faculties in Mad School who will help you in each stage from ideation to execution to presentation of your original ideas in the most effective visual manner. We extend support and help to students in preparing both the physical and digital format of the portfolio.

Mock tests play an important role in preparing for entrance exams. Benefits of conducting mock tests include understanding the question paper pattern, improving speed and building confidence to make sure you are exam ready. At Mad School, mock tests are conducted in 2 formats that ensure effective learning during the coaching. Topic wise tests are conducted every week and tests are conducted module wise at the end of every month. Mock test series is conducted everyday after completing the syllabus. Tests are conducted for students who have enrolled in both offline and online coaching programs. Online mock test series (one mock test is free) are available on our website for any one who wishes to test their level of preparation. Students who purchase mock test series from our website will gain free access to online support and doubts clearing session from our faculties.

Yes, Mad School has a series of books for each entrance exam such as NIFT, NID, NATA, CEED, UCEED, and others as well. All study materials are shared with the students post completion of admission formalities of our coaching centre. Study materials are also available for online purchase at affordable prices.
