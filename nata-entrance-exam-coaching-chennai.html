NATA Entrance Exam 2024

Understanding the ins and outs…


NATA stands for National Aptitude Test in Architecture. It is a competitive exam conducted all across India for admissions to undergraduate architecture programs. This national-level exam is conducted by the Council of Architecture (COA).

NATA exam is conducted to evaluate the candidate's


  • Architectural aptitude
  • Drawing
  • Observation skills
  • Critical thinking
  • Aesthetic sensitivity

NATA scores are accepted by various architecture colleges across India as a fundamental criterion for admission to their B.Arch. programs.

There are other exams such as JEE2, CEPT, and many more that are similar to NATA that are accepted by particular colleges in India.


NATA Exam Eligibility 2024:


There is no age limit when it comes to attempting the NATA exam.


NATA Exam Pattern 2024:


  • Mode of test for NATA exam 2024 is online (Computer-based test).
  • Duration of the NATA exam 2024 is 3 hours.
  • The NATA exam has one section that tests general aptitude.
  • Total marks awarded for the NATA exam is 200.
  • Total number of questions asked in the NATA exam is 125.
  • There are no negative markings in the NATA exam 2024.

The NATA exam pattern was changed in 2020.

Even though the COA (Council of Architecture) changes the exam pattern periodically, there was a major change in the NATA exam pattern for the 2020 batch.

The new NATA exam pattern focuses more on the aptitude section and the drawing section was removed from the exam. These changes were made to make the exam more aligned with the present curriculum and learning outcomes of the B.Arch program. Another objective of these changes was to make the test more accessible to students from various disciplines, whether science or commerce.


NATA 2024 Attempts:


Students are allowed to appear for up to 3 NATA tests.

  • Case 1:
    • If the candidate appears for 1 test, that particular score will be taken into consideration.
  • Case 2:
    • If the candidate appears for 2 tests, the best of 2 scores will be taken into consideration.
  • Case 3:
    • If the candidate appears for all 3 tests, the average of the 2 best scores will be taken into consideration.

FAQs

Find your answers…


1. What is NATA exam?

A: The National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA) is a national-level entrance exam that is the minimum requirement for admission to an undergraduate architecture course in India. The Council of Architecture (CoA) conducts the exam, and its scores are accepted by most of the top architecture colleges in the country.

2. Is coaching necessary for Nata examination?

A: NATA coaching is not essential if you have a strong foundation in architecture and are good at PCM. However, it can be helpful to get coaching if you want to get the best possible score in the exam. Coaching can provide you with the right guidance and practice based on the latest exam trends. It can also be beneficial for students with time constraints, as it can help them to improve their weaker areas in a short amount of time.

3. When will the NATA 2024 dates be announced?

A: The NATA 2024 exam dates have not yet been released. The application process usually opens in September/October of the previous year, and the exam dates are announced in December/January of the same year. So, the NATA 2024 exam dates are expected to be announced sometime between December 2023 and January 2024.

To be updated about the dates and latest notifications, you can visit the “All you need to know about NATA” page or the official NATA website https://nata.in/

4. Can I appear for JEE2 and CEPT if I am attempting NATA?

A: Yes, you can take the JEE2 and CEPT exams along with the NATA exam. However, it is important to be well-prepared for all of the exams.

5. Does NATA have negative marking?

A: No, the 2024 NATA exam does not have negative marking.

6. Is NATA compulsory for architecture?

A: Yes! The NATA score is the primary factor considered for admission to architecture colleges. Your higher secondary examination marks are also taken into account during the admission process.

7. Is NATA exam tough?

A: The NATA exam is a fair exam. It is neither too easy nor too difficult. The NATA exam is a good test of your aptitude for architecture. If you are strong in math, spatial reasoning, and drawing, you will find the exam to be manageable.
