About NIFT Courses

The NIFT (National Institute of Fashion Technology) is a place where every designer pictures themselves studying. NIFT courses are designed in such a way that it brings out the creativity of the students and encourages them to progress in the dynamically changing fashion industry. This is what makes NIFT different from other design colleges.

There are multiple specializations that NIFT offers starting from fashion designing to fashion technology and more. You have to choose the right specialization which is fit for you.

If you find yourself stuck and need free career counseling. Call 9959750006 to get free career counseling from experts at Mad School.


There are NIFT colleges located in 18 states and UTs. There are a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma courses available that each NIFT college has to offer.
